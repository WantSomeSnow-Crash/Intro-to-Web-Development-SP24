Who am I

I am a New York-based photographer and a former student at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts. My photography does not have a singular focus. Instead, I pride myself on capturing moments reflective of the nuances of life. I strive to showcase the unexpected and overlooked with the hope of giving importance to things that are taken for granted.
